# Content Production Pipeline

## Pipeline

1. Collect sources and keep source links.
2. Normalize into structured notes: date, theme, location, source, fact, recommendation, caveat.
3. Remove duplicates and mark uncertain claims.
4. Draft the artifact in the requested shape.
5. Verify the artifact visually or structurally.
6. Report the deliverable path and checks.

## Common Deliverables

- Travel itinerary: organize by day, city, transport, accommodation, play, cash, risks.
- PPT: build slide structure first, then add images, links, and notes; verify preview and links.
- Xiaohongshu research summary: include original links, visible metrics, and actionable takeaways.
- Video or tutorial script: break into hook, sections, visuals, captions, and verification notes.

## Verification Checklist

- Sources: links are present and not invented.
- Structure: every user-provided item is placed somewhere or explicitly marked unresolved.
- Visuals: screenshots or previews have been inspected when layout matters.
- Links: hyperlink counts or representative clickable links have been verified.
- Output: saved under an agreed output directory, not only described in chat.

## Reporting Style

Keep final reports short:

- What was made
- Where it is
- What was checked
- What to inspect next
